
Question 1
Of the following, which is the best approximation to the probability that a permutation of size N has exactly 2 cycles?

Answer:-
1/N


Question 2
Which event is least likely, in a random permutation of size 1000?

Answer:-
It has no cycles of length less than 5.

Additional Question: Which event is most likely, in a random permutation of size 1000?
Annswer:- it has no cycles of length 1, 2, 3 or 7.
